How it works?

As we have already said, helping is one of our main tasks. All the money that you donate, with which you buy treats for animals, we send all this to help our collaborating zoos and their animals. And if one of the animals you especially liked, you can take him under guardianship. Any, even a very small contribution, will make his living conditions much better, and you will have a new friend who you can chat with at any time!
